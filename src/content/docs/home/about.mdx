<Card title="komichi means “small path” in Japanese">A quiet, intentional trail that invites mastery one step at a time.</Card>

This repository is a curated pathway for developers who want to grow consistently and deliberately, exploring concepts,
hands‑on exercises, and structured learning journeys.
It is designed to be minimal, calm, and focused:
```
    a place where progress happens through steady motion.
```

---

## Philosophy

komichi is built around three principles:

<Card title="Small Steps">Deep learning doesn’t require overwhelming leaps. It thrives on gentle, repeatable progression.</Card>
<Card title="Clarity Over Noise">Modern dev ecosystems can be chaotic. komichi filters out the noise and keeps only what moves you forward.</Card>
<Card title="Craft Through Practice">Mastery isn’t theoretical — it is formed through repetition, reflection, and applied understanding.</Card>
